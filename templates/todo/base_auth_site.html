Special, not deletable lists
@inbox: when you delete a list, all todo are moved to @inbox.
@hot: a list containing all todo with a priority OR a due date in the past OR within the next 7 days.
@trash: when you delete a todo, it is moved into @trash list. You can un-delete the todo by clicking on the icon.
